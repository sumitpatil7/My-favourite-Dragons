Balerion

Balerion the Black Dread

The Colossus of Valyria and Conqueror's Flame, Ridden by Legends

Balerion, the Black Dread, was the largest and most terrifying dragon to ever exist. His black scales and fiery breath symbolized destruction, as seen when he reduced Harrenhal to ash during Aegon’s conquest. A creature of immense power, his shadow could darken entire towns, and his legacy remains unmatched among dragons.

Drogon

Daenerys's fierce and loyal dragon, symbolizing her strength and power.

Drogon is the largest and most powerful of Daenerys's dragons, with black scales and red eyes. He plays a key role in her rise to power, often turning the tide of battle with his fiery breath. Drogon helps Daenerys defeat her enemies, most notably in key battles where his strength crushes opposing forces. Beyond his physical power, Drogon shares a deep bond with Daenerys, offering her both protection and emotional support as she navigates her journey to reclaim her birthright.

Aegon Targaryen

The Prince that was promised. Ice and Fire.

Jon Snow, trueborn Aegon Targaryen, carries the weight of ancient blood—both the fire of the Targaryens and the stoic resolve of the Starks. His legacy is one of prophecy, as the "Prince That Was Promised," born to unite and save the world from darkness. With the blood of dragons in his veins, he inherits not only the Targaryen's legacy of power but also their tragic fate, bound to a destiny shaped by both love and sacrifice. His name, though born in secret, echoes the eternal struggle between duty and desire, honor and ambition, marking him as a force destined to change the course of history.

Vhagar

Ancient, colossal, and fearsome embodiment of destruction.

Vhagar, one of Westeros' most legendary dragons, symbolized raw power and destruction. First ridden by Queen Visenya Targaryen during Aegon’s Conquest, she played a key role in uniting the Seven Kingdoms. By the Dance of the Dragons, she was the largest and oldest living dragon, ridden by Prince Aemond Targaryen. Her battles, especially her final clash with Caraxes over the God’s Eye, showcased her unmatched ferocity. Though victorious, Vhagar perished, leaving a legacy of power, destruction, and the tragic cost of ambition.

Rhaenyra & Daemon

A Targaryen bond.

Rhaenyra, the “Black Queen”, symbolized resilience, fighting for her right to rule despite a patriarchal society. Her pursuit of the throne ignited the Dance of the Dragons, a devastating civil war that weakened Westeros and nearly wiped out the dragons. Their legacy lives on as both an inspiration and a cautionary tale of ambition’s cost. Daemon and Rhaenyra Targaryen left a legacy of ambition, defiance, and tragedy. Daemon, the Rogue Prince, was a fearless warrior and dragonrider whose exploits and rebellion made him both hero and outcast. His marriage to Rhaenyra united them in their shared goal to secure her claim to the Iron Throne.
